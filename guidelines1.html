Hostel Guidelines

Boys' Hostel Guidelines

General Rules and Regulations

  • Maintain cleanliness in your personal and common areas.
  • Follow the hostel’s designated quiet hours from 10 PM to 6 AM.
  • Personal belongings should be stored properly and not obstruct common areas.
  • Respect the hostel’s no-smoking policy inside the premises.
  • Use of electrical appliances like heaters or kettles is regulated and requires prior approval.
  • All hostel guests must be registered at the front desk.
  • Report any damages or maintenance issues immediately to the hostel management.
  • Abide by the hostel’s check-in and check-out timings.

Allowed

  • Personal electronics such as laptops, tablets, and phones.
  • Food items in designated areas like the dining hall or common room.
  • Participation in hostel-organized events and activities.

Not Allowed

  • Guests in hostel rooms without prior permission.
  • Creating loud noise during quiet hours (10 PM - 6 AM).
  • Consumption of alcohol or drugs on the premises.
  • Unauthorized use of high-power electrical appliances.

Fines

  • Unauthorized guests: 500 Rs
  • Alcohol/drug possession: 1000 Rs
  • Unauthorized electrical appliance use: 200 Rs

Girls' Hostel Guidelines

General Rules and Regulations

  • Keep personal and common areas clean and organized.
  • Adhere to the quiet hours policy from 10 PM to 6 AM.
  • Store personal belongings in designated areas to avoid clutter.
  • Smoking is prohibited within the hostel premises.
  • Use of certain electrical appliances requires approval from the hostel management.
  • Visitors must be registered and are not allowed in rooms without permission.
  • Report maintenance issues or damages to the hostel management promptly.
  • Follow the hostel's check-in and check-out schedules.

Allowed

  • Personal electronics such as laptops, tablets, and phones.
  • Food items in specified areas such as the dining hall or kitchen.
  • Participation in various hostel activities and events.

Not Allowed

  • Unauthorized visitors in the rooms.
  • Making excessive noise during quiet hours (10 PM - 6 AM).
  • Consumption of alcohol or drugs within the hostel.
  • Use of high-power electrical appliances without permission.

Fines

  • Unauthorized guests: 500 Rs
  • Alcohol/drug possession: 1000 Rs
  • Unauthorized electrical appliance use: 200 Rs
