Role of Admin in Hostel Management

As an admin in the Hostel Management System, your key responsibilities include:

  • Managing Student Information: Adding, updating, and maintaining student records.
  • Room Allocation: Assigning and managing room allocations for students.
  • Notice Management: Posting important notices and updates to inform students.
  • Handling Applications: Reviewing and processing student applications and requests.
  • Resolving Issues: Addressing and resolving issues or complaints raised by students.

Ensure efficient hostel operations and provide students with a smooth experience.
