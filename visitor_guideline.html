Visitor's Guidelines

Guidelines for Visitors

  • All visitors must register at the reception upon arrival.
  • Visitors must carry a valid ID.
  • Visiting hours are from 9 AM to 6 PM.
  • Maintain decorum and respect the premises.
  • Unauthorized access to restricted areas is prohibited.
  • Smoking and alcohol consumption are not allowed.
  • Please follow the instructions of the security staff.
